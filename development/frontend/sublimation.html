What is Dye Sublimation?

Dye sublimation uses heat and pressure to turn solid dye into gas, which bonds directly with polyester materials and specially coated products at a molecular level. This creates permanent, vibrant prints that become part of the material itself.

1

Design Printed

Your design is printed in reverse using sublimation inks onto transfer paper

2

Heat Applied

Transfer paper placed on item, subjected to 375-400°F heat and pressure

3

Permanent Bond

Dye becomes gas, penetrates material and becomes permanently embedded
