What Are UV DTF Stickers?

UV DTF (Direct to Film) Stickers are full-color, high-resolution decals printed using UV-curable inks on a release film, then transferred to hard surfaces without needing heat or water. They provide a raised, glossy texture that feels premium and permanent.

1

UV Printing

Design printed with UV-curable inks onto PET film and instantly cured

2

Peel & Stick

Remove backing film, place on surface, and apply pressure

3

Final Result

Peel away top film leaving durable, textured graphic
