About PrintGuys

Canada's premier DTF printing and custom apparel specialists

Our Story

PrintGuys was founded with a simple mission: to provide Canada with the highest quality custom printing services at transparent, fair prices. What started as a passion for perfect prints has grown into Canada's leading DTF printing operation.

We specialize in Direct-to-Film (DTF) heat transfers, but our expertise extends across all custom printing methods including screen printing, embroidery, sublimation, and large format printing.

From single-piece orders to large production runs, we treat every project with the same attention to detail and commitment to quality.
