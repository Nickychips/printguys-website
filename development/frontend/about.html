About PrintGuys

Canada's premier DTF printing and custom apparel production company. From concept to completion, we bring your vision to life with precision and passion.

At PrintGuys, we're more than just a print shop — we're Canada's premier DTF printing specialist and your complete creative production partner. Specializing in direct-to-film transfers (DTF), screen printing, embroidery, sublimation, and large format printing, we bring ideas to life with precision, passion, and professional care.

Our mission is simple: deliver high-quality custom printing with transparent pricing, no minimum orders, and lightning-fast turnaround. We serve businesses, artists, entrepreneurs, and creators across Canada who need reliable, fast, and detail-oriented print solutions.

What sets PrintGuys apart is our commitment to consistency, creativity, and collaboration. We treat every order — from 1 piece to 10,000 — with the same level of importance and attention to detail. Our team works closely with each client to ensure their vision is realized exactly as imagined.

Whether you need DTF heat transfers, custom embroidery, or screen printing, we're here to help you make a lasting impression — because at PrintGuys, your vision is our job.
