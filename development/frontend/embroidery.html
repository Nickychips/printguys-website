What is Custom Embroidery?

Custom embroidery uses computerized machines to stitch thread directly into fabric, creating raised, textured designs that are permanent and professional. This time-tested method produces the most durable and prestigious branding solution available.

1

Design Digitized

Your logo converted to embroidery format with stitch patterns

3

4

Quality Check

Final inspection and trimming for professional finish
